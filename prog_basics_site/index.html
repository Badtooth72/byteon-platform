How to use this site

  1. Choose a topic from the menu above.
  2. Read the Explanation and study the Worked Example.
  3. Complete the Easy and Harder tasks in the code section of that page.
  4. Once your program works, copy and paste your code into OneNote to show what you’ve achieved.
  5. Try the Quiz at the bottom of each topic page.
  6. Your topic button will change colour to show your score:
    🟩 Green = 100% 🟧 Amber ≥ 50% 🟥 Red < 50%
  7. When all topics are complete, take the Final Quiz to test everything together.
